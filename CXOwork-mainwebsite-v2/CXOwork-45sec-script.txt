CXOwork — 45-Second Explainer Video Script
==========================================

[0:00–0:08] — Hook
Background: CXOwork homepage hero

"Most early-stage startups don't need a full-time CFO, CTO, or CMO.
But they desperately need that expertise — right now."

---

[0:08–0:18] — Problem
Background: Founder dashboard, empty C-suite slots

"Hiring a senior executive takes months, costs hundreds of thousands
a year, and most of the time — it's the wrong fit."

---

[0:18–0:30] — Solution / How it works
Background: CXOwork matching flow UI, advisor profiles

"CXOwork connects you with vetted fractional executives in under
48 hours. Tell us your stage, your gap, your goal — and we match
you with someone who's done it before, at companies just like yours."

---

[0:30–0:40] — Results
Background: Testimonial cards / metrics ("Series A closed", "70% faster deploys")

"Our founders close funding rounds faster, fix GTM in weeks, and
scale without chaos — for a fraction of the cost."

---

[0:40–0:45] — CTA
Background: Get Started page / CXOwork logo

"Get matched today. Your fractional CXO is waiting."

---

----------------
